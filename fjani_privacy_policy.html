Privacy Policy for Fjáni

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Fjáni ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Information

Fjáni requests access to your device's location services to calculate accurate sunrise and sunset times for your area. You can choose to:

  • Grant permission to use your GPS location, or
  • Manually enter a city name instead

Important: Location data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties. We do not store your precise location coordinates.

App Preferences

The app stores the following preferences locally on your device:

  • Notification settings (enabled/disabled, advance warning time)
  • Display preferences (language selection, timer display options)
  • Location preference (GPS or manual city entry)

This information is stored only on your device using local storage and is never transmitted externally.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app will send you reminders about flag-raising times. Notification permissions are managed through your device's operating system settings. We do not collect or store any information about your notification interactions.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  1. Calculating Sunrise/Sunset Times: Location data is used exclusively to determine accurate sunrise and sunset times for your area.
  2. Providing Notifications: Notification preferences are used to send you timely reminders about flag-raising and flag-lowering times.
  3. App Functionality: Stored preferences allow the app to remember your settings and provide a personalized experience.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage mechanisms provided by your operating system.
  • We do not maintain any servers or databases that store your personal information.
  • Location data is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored.
  • App preferences are encrypted using your device's built-in security features.

Third-Party Services

Fjáni uses the following third-party services that are essential for app functionality:

  • Expo Framework: The app is built using Expo, which provides the development framework. Expo does not collect personal data from end users.
  • Device Operating System: The app uses your device's native location services and notification systems, which are governed by your device's privacy settings.

We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or any other third-party services that collect or track your personal information.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal information to any third parties. All data remains on your device and is never sent to external servers.

Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Location Access: You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings. The app will continue to function with manual city entry.
  • Notifications: You can disable notifications at any time through the app settings or your device's notification settings.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app from your device. Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling the app will permanently remove all stored preferences.

Children's Privacy

Fjáni is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

International Users

Fjáni is designed to work globally. If you are using the app from outside your home country, please be aware that your information may be processed in accordance with your device's local privacy laws.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • GitHub Repository: https://github.com/jongud/fjani
  • Issues: Please open an issue on our GitHub repository for privacy-related inquiries

Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Apple App Store Privacy Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles

Summary

Fjáni is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Location data is used only for calculating sunrise/sunset times
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No analytics or tracking
  • You have full control over your data

Effective Date: This Privacy Policy is effective as of December 2024 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future, which will take effect immediately upon being posted on this page.
